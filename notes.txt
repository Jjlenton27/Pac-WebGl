web based/embedded
pacman required features:
	rendering
	2 axis movement
	player control
	collision/position control
	dots/power up generation & placement
	dots/powerup pickup
	ghost-player collision and death
	game end condition
	start/end menus
	respawn & lives


use webgl for rendering, allows embedding (https://webgl2fundamentals.org, https://www.tutorialspoint.com/webgl/webgl_basics.htm, https://adammurray.blog/webgl/tutorials/triangle/, http://www.webglacademy.com/courses.php?courses=0_1_20_2_3_4_23_5_6_7_10#1)
